The work

Where the research comes from.

Each year, we publish an annual market study, quarterly Category Compass reports, and bespoke client research. The work is built on surveys with thousands of senior HR and TA leaders. The Human-Centric AI Council adds practitioner-led work on AI. Our edge is pairing primary research with rigorous secondary analysis, because neither catches the full signal alone.

Get involved

Three ways in.

Read.

Subscribe to the newsletter. New research and event access land in your inbox. We send when there's something to say. No noise. Just signal.

Show up.

Town halls, webinars, and working sessions are open to the list. Bring a question and you'll get one back. Replies and comments go to the team, not a bot. The conversation is the point.

Contribute.

Submit a topic you'd want researched. Apply to the Council if you're shaping AI in your org. Get featured in the practitioner spotlight when you've got a story worth sharing. The work is better when practitioners help write it.
