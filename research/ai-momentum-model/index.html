What it is

Where your org actually stands.

The AI Momentum Model maps where HR organizations are in their AI journey across three dimensions: Capability, Posture, and Investment. Most readiness frameworks tell you what good looks like. This one tells you where you are.

The nine archetypes that emerge from the research give teams a shared language for their situation — and a clear view of what has to change before momentum builds.

The three dimensions
Capability
Are we equipped with the foundations to apply AI in HR?
AI Literacy Baseline fluency across the function, not just leaders.
Governance Policies and oversight that don't stall the work.
Integration Connected systems and data, not siloed point tools.
Posture
Are we committed and aligned on how to move?
Strategy A real plan, not endless exploration.
Ownership Named owners and cross-functional partners.
Risk Orientation Embrace with guardrails, not avoidance.
Investment
Are we committing meaningful resources?
Funding Budget that scales beyond a pilot.
People Dedicated capacity, not side-of-desk work.
Scope Use cases broad enough to matter.
