What makes us different.

Four things we do that most firms don't. Each one shows up in the work, not just the pitch.

01
Built with HR practitioners.

Our team has run the function we research. Surveys reach thousands of senior HR and TA leaders. The Human-Centric AI Council adds practitioner-led work on AI specifically.

02
Contrarian when the consensus is wrong.

When the AI Interviewer category was new, we benchmarked solutions instead of ranking them. We said the market wasn't ready, on the record. Safe takes don't move the function forward.

We don't paywall research. Vendor underwriting and advisory engagements fund the work, so practitioners read every report without hitting a gate. Free is a model, not a giveaway.

The Human-Centric AI Council

The HCAIC, by the practitioners doing the work.

The HCAIC is a working group of HR and TA practitioners building peer-led resources for the rest of the function on AI. Members write, review, and ship their own contributions.

Underwriters get visibility around the Council. Not access to its members. The Council does not exist to be a buyer panel.
