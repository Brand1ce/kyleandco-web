The council led by the HR leaders successfully implementing AI.

The HCAIC is a working group of HR and TA practitioners building peer-led resources for the rest of the function on AI. Members write, review, and ship their own contributions.

Underwriters get structured access to members for product feedback and practitioner analysis. That access is built for sharpening products and learning from the function. The Council stays a working group, never a buyer panel.
