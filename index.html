The Human-Centric AI Council

The HCAIC.

The HCAIC is a working group of HR and TA practitioners building peer-led resources for the rest of the function on AI. Members shape what good AI literacy, governance, and adoption look like in practice. A senior steering committee guides what the Council takes on and how the work goes out.

The work isn't a panel of names on a slide. Members write, review, and ship their own contributions. Town halls, toolkits, working sessions, frameworks. All of it built by practitioners, for practitioners. The firm provides the analyst layer, the editorial discipline, and the distribution. Members keep the work honest to the work.

Underwriters get visibility around the Council, not access to its members. The Council does not exist to be a buyer panel. It exists because the people doing the work should have a room to talk in.
