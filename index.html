What sets us apart

What makes us different.

Four things we do that most firms don't. Each one shows up in the work, not just the pitch.

01
Built with HR practitioners.

Our team has run the function we research. Surveys reach thousands of senior HR and TA leaders. The Human-Centric AI Council adds practitioner-led work on AI specifically.

02
Contrarian when the consensus is wrong.

We publish what the evidence shows. That means saying when a category isn't ready to rank, naming what isn't working when nobody else will, and refusing to publish reactive takes that breed fear and confusion. The function deserves measured calls, not reactive ones.

03
Generous by design.

We aim for the least friction between practitioners and the research they need. Our goal is to keep substantive research within reach of every leader who could use it. No noise, just signal.

04
We stay in the room.

Most firms publish and walk. Kyle & Co stays close with briefings, workshops, and evaluation frameworks. We help practitioners read the market, evaluate tools, and make calls they can defend.

Working together

Four ways to work with us.

HR-tech vendors and HR and TA teams both hire us. The four shapes below flex to fit either side.

01
Underwrite the work.

Underwriting is a multi-quarter partnership built around the AI Council. Your brand gets associated with the practitioner-led work on AI that HR leaders are turning to, and your team gets the kind of access and relationships that only come with sustained presence in the room.

Long-term partnership
02
Drive a moment.

Sometimes the partnership you need is built around a single moment. A product launch, a market entry, a conference, or a strategy rollout where you need to land with the right audience. We design bounded engagements scoped to that moment, with deliverables and a timeline matched to what the work requires.

Launch & activation
03
Build the research.

When you need original research to validate a market claim, map a category, sharpen positioning, or back an internal initiative with data your team can defend, we build it with you. The work takes the shape of a custom study, a Category Compass collaboration, a messaging audit, or a focus group, depending on the question you're trying to answer.

Research & data
04
Get a thinking partner.

Sometimes what you need isn't a deliverable, it's a thinking partner. Strategic advisory hours, product advisory, and executive workshops give you analyst access for the moments when you're sizing a bet, refining positioning, or working through a hard call you don't want to make alone.

The Human-Centric AI Council

The HCAIC, by the practitioners doing the work.

The HCAIC is a working group of HR and TA practitioners building peer-led resources for the rest of the function on AI. Members write, review, and ship their own contributions.

Underwriters get visibility around the Council. Not access to its members. The Council does not exist to be a buyer panel.
